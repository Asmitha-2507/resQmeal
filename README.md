## 🧠 AI-Powered Food Waste Prevention & Rescue Platform

ResQMeal is an intelligent platform designed to reduce food waste and fight hunger by connecting surplus food donors with NGOs and volunteers in real time. Unlike traditional food donation platforms, ResQMeal leverages Artificial Intelligence to predict surplus food, assess food safety, optimize distribution, and ensure that edible food reaches the right people before it goes to waste.

## 🚨 Problem Statement

Millions of tons of edible food are discarded every day by restaurants, hotels, hostels, supermarkets, and event organizers, while many people struggle with food insecurity. Existing solutions primarily focus on food donation but lack intelligent decision-making, predictive analytics, and efficient coordination.

## 💡 Our Solution

ResQMeal transforms food rescue into an AI-driven ecosystem that not only redistributes surplus food but also helps prevent food waste before it happens.

* 🍽️ Surplus Food Donation
